Sponsorship Prospectus

What is rovercode?

Business Objective

rovercode seeks to provide all teachers with an affordable, zero-fuss student robotics system that allows their students to be coding within minutes.

Core Principals

  • Open source benefits everyone. Everyone is welcome to use and improve all of rovercode's hardware and software.
  • Students should hit the ground coding. While many educational robotics platforms emphasize construction, rovercode will provide a ready-to-code-out-of-the-box product. At the same time, rovercode's open-source nature means there will be no limit or black boxes for any student interested in building a rover from scratch.
  • All students can learn to code. Coding doesn't take "a special kind of brain" to learn; rather, anyone can learn it with appropriate instruction and experiences.
